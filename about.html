About Us

Fuel Importation & Logistics Between the U.S. and Mexico

Petroalca LLC is a binational energy company specializing in fuel importation, storage, and logistics operations connecting the United States and Mexico.

With an operational capacity of up to 30 million liters per day of gasoline and ULSD diesel, we ensure compliance with NOM-016 standards, transparency, and efficiency in every delivery.

Our infrastructure includes strategic terminals, barge operations, and logistics routes that guarantee timely, secure, and sustainable fuel supply for our partners.

We offer our transportation and logistics services to ensure the safe and efficient delivery of fuels to PMI Pemex. Our team is ready to discuss all the details and conditions of our proposal to meet your operational needs.

Our Strength

Infrastructure and Resources

Petroalca LLC operates on a robust 125-hectare energy and logistics complex strategically located along the U.S.–Mexico border in La Barranca, Tamaulipas, with 500 meters of direct access to the Los Indios International Bridge in San Benito, TX.

Our Harlingen, TX terminal is equipped with a 500,000-barrel storage capacity and maintains a continuous inventory of over 100 million liters of gasoline and ULSD diesel. This infrastructure is supported by a multimodal logistics network that integrates maritime, rail, and road transport for efficient and uninterrupted supply.

  • Strategic terminal access through Los Indios International Bridge.
  • Large-scale storage and distribution capacity for continuous operations.
  • Multimodal logistics integrating port, road, and rail transport.
  • 24/7 operations ensuring safety, compliance, and timely delivery.
