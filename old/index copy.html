About Us

Petroalca LLC – Fuel Commercialization & Logistics

Petroalca LLC is a binational company dedicated to the import and logistics of fuels between the United States and Mexico.

Our operational capacity reaches up to 30 million liters per day of gasoline and ULSD diesel, fully compliant with NOM-016 standards.

We operate under principles of transparency, legality, and alignment with national energy policy, ensuring safe and efficient delivery at every stage.

Our Strength

Infrastructure and Resources

We operate on 125 hectares along the U.S.–Mexico border in La Barranca, Tamaulipas, with 500 meters of frontage at the Los Indios International Bridge in San Benito, TX. Our Harlingen, TX terminal provides a 500,000-barrel capacity and maintains a permanent inventory of over 100 million liters ready for delivery. Supported by barges and a multimodal logistics network, we ensure efficient maritime, ground, and last-mile transport.

Strategic Operation Points & Logistics Routes

Our logistics network is designed to ensure efficient, secure, and timely fuel transport between key border terminals and national distribution routes across Mexico and the United States.

Strategic Operation Points
  • Harlingen, TX: Main terminal with 500,000-barrel capacity.
  • Los Indios, TX: 125-hectare property for a future exclusive terminal to Mexico.
  • Pharr / Reynosa: Border crossing with direct access to national transport routes.
  • Brownsville / Matamoros (Los Tomates): Alternative corridor with overweight route access.
Operational Routes
  • Harlingen Port → Los Indios Bridge (15 min)
  • Harlingen → Pharr / Reynosa (45 min)
  • Brownsville → Los Tomates (30 min)
