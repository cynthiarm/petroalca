About Us

Fuel Importation & Logistics Between the U.S. and Mexico

Petroalca LLC is a binational energy company specializing in fuel importation, storage, and logistics operations connecting the United States and Mexico.

With an operational capacity of up to 30 million liters per day of gasoline and ULSD diesel, we ensure compliance with NOM-016 standards, transparency, and efficiency in every delivery.

Our infrastructure includes strategic terminals, barge operations, and logistics routes that guarantee timely, secure, and sustainable fuel supply for our partners.

Our Strength

Infrastructure and Resources

We operate on 125 hectares along the U.S.–Mexico border in La Barranca, Tamaulipas, with 500 meters of frontage at the Los Indios International Bridge in San Benito, TX. Our Harlingen, TX terminal offers a 500,000-barrel capacity and maintains a permanent inventory of over 100 million liters ready for delivery. Supported by barges and a multimodal logistics network, we ensure efficient maritime, ground, and last-mile transport.

Strategic Operation Points & Logistics Routes

Petroalca’s logistics network ensures safe, efficient, and on-time fuel transportation between U.S.–Mexico border terminals and national distribution corridors.

Strategic Operation Points
  • Harlingen, TX: Main terminal with 500,000-barrel capacity.
  • Los Indios, TX: 125-hectare site for future exclusive terminal to Mexico.
  • Pharr / Reynosa: Border crossing with direct access to national transport routes.
  • Brownsville / Matamoros (Los Tomates): Alternative logistics corridor with overweight route access.
Operational Routes
  • Harlingen Port → Los Indios Bridge (15 min)
  • Harlingen → Pharr / Reynosa (45 min)
